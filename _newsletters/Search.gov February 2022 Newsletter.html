Search.gov Year in-Review

The searches that shaped 2021

We are pleased to share with you our 2021 Year In Review. This report explores the top needs of 2021, expressed in the public’s own words. For each of the 18 topics, this report provides the top 25 searches in that topic, trend data since 2019, and the top websites serving the public in that area.

2021 in numbers:

  • 286.5 million searches
  • 2,100+ participating gov websites
  • 18 top topics
  • 365 days of connecting people with what they need

2021 top five search topics across government websites: 

  • Benefits
  • Loans
  • Space
  • Immigration
  • Forms
