### The searches that shaped 2020 and our system highlights

</ul>
<span style="font-size: 16px;">System Highlights:

<ul>
<li aria-level="1" style="-webkit-text-size-adjust:100%; -ms-text-size-adjust:100%"><span style="font-size: 16px;">Released a new Clicks Tracking API to enable analytics capture for Results API users
<li aria-level="1" style="-webkit-text-size-adjust:100%; -ms-text-size-adjust:100%"><span style="font-size: 16px;">Updated our Login.gov integration for a consistent experience through login &amp; logout

<li aria-level="1" style="-webkit-text-size-adjust:100%; -ms-text-size-adjust:100%"><span style="font-size: 16px;">Added backend indexing improvements (reindexing support and invalid sitemap/URL handling)
