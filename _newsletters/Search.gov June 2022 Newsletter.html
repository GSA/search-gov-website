Your Metadata Matters!

Help Improve Your Search Experience

Metadata is data information that helps search engines choose which websites come up first when you type a word or phrase into a search engine. Simply said, metadata tells search engines how to read and display sites on search engine result pages (SERPs). To optimize your metadata for search engines, we recommend to focus on your website’s title tag and meta descriptions. Read more details about the metadata and tags you should include in your website.
